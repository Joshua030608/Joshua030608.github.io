About Warrior E-Bikes

The name Warrior E-Bikes originates from the H.B. Middle School Mascot. Our founding members are Annamay Hendrixson, Jacob Ford, Joshua Ford, and Sean Eppehimer. We currently have 3 outstanding E-Bikes for sale which are loved around the world. We have a strong passion for biking and firmly believe that E-bikes are going to change biking for the better.
